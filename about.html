Our Story

Caring for pets since 2022.

Dedicated to Every Tail & Whisker

Golden Paws Pet Shop was founded in 2022 by a family of pet lovers who noticed a need for a modern, high-quality pet boutique in Colombo. What started as a small dream has grown into a community hub for animal enthusiasts.

We believe that pets are more than just animals—they are members of the family. That's why we hand-select every product on our shelves, from nutritious foods to durable toys and cozy bedding.

Our Mission

To provide Colombo's pet owners with premium, healthy, and ethical pet supplies while offering expert support to ensure every pet lives their best, happiest life.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and beloved pet shop in Sri Lanka, known for our uncompromising quality and our "pets-first" philosophy.
